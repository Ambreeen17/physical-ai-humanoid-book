## Agent Orchestration Pipeline

### 10-Agent Pipeline for Each Chapter
1. **Research Agent**: Analyzes chapter topic and produces research notes
2. **Author Agent**: Creates main chapter content
3. **Diagram Agent**: Generates diagrams and visual aids
4. **Lab Agent**: Creates Docker-based lab environments
5. **Assessment Agent**: Designs quizzes and challenges
6. **Personalization Agent**: Creates difficulty variants
7. **Localization Agent**: Translates content to Urdu
8. **RAG Indexing Agent**: Prepares vector embeddings
9. **QA Agent**: Validates quality and consistency
10. **File Management**: Organizes artifacts
